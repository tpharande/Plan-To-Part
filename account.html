Account Questions



I signed up, but I haven't received my confirmation email.


You can sign in again on the log in page or notify us at plantopart.com.


Can I still have a Plan To Part if I'm not from the United States?


We are currently in the US but if you notify us with your email we can let you know when we will be available internationally.


Why am I asked for a phone number to sign up?


It is needed to verify your identity and also to send a verification code to complete two- factor security verification.


How do I reset my password?


Use the set reset password on the login page.


Why am I unable to login to my account?


Make sure you entered the correct user name and password. If you continue to have problems with the log in you can notify customer service at plantopart.com.
