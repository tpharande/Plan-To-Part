Billing Questions



What's included in a Plan To Part subscription?


The subscription includes 24/7 access to add or change any information, download, edit or remove documents or any additional requests you have specified. You can prints any and all reports and access the help any section and chat bot.


How much does a Plan To Part cost?


There is an annual fee of $49 dollars which provides full access of all reports and storage of documents


How does billing work?


You will be asked to put a credit card on file and will automatically be billed annually.


If I stop paying for my account, what happens to my Plan To Part?


You will be notified and giving 30 days to print out any reports before you account and all information is deleted.


How do I cancel my subscription?


Just notify us 30 days in advance at plantopart.com with a request to terminate you account and you will be billed only for the portion of the year the account was active.
