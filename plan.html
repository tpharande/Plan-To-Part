Plan Questions



I've created an account, but I'm not sure how to get started.


It’s easy! Just start by filling out the questions and the program will guide you through the six sections to provide you with a complete report of your expressed wishes. If you have any questions please consult the help page or the automated Chat tool.


I forgot my password. What should I do?


It’s simple just reset you password on the log in page.


How do I print or download my Plan To Part?


There is a report generation that will allow you to either print each of the 6 sections independently or allow you to print a full report.


Can I change the order in which my data is presented in my Plan to Part?


You cannot change the presentation of the full report but each of the six sections can be printed independently.


How do I import my financial account information from my bank?


The information from the bank will need to be inputted manually.


Can other people edit my Plan To Part?


There will be six sections and you can give access to one additional person for each section or give one person access to the entire report.
